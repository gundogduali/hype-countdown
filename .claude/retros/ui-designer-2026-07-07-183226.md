## What I found in review
1. `get_screenshot` returned a completely empty image several times right after batch_design (fonts load asynchronously); `snapshot_layout` also gave half-computed inconsistent coordinates / false "clipped" warnings at the same time. The design was actually sound.
2. The `note` node doesn't support the `textGrowth` property — I got a batch error on the first attempt.
3. `text-muted` (#7A9A80) had ~2.8:1 contrast on the beige background (insufficient for 13px footnotes).

## What I fixed
1. On the empty screenshot I did not start redesigning; I verified the data with `batch_get` and retook the screenshot (cross-confirmed with a document-level image). In the final round all 6 screens were re-verified one by one — no overflow/alignment issues.
2. Gave the note fixed width/height and removed textGrowth.
3. `text-muted` → #5E7C65 (via the token, fixed across all screens at once).
